# Usage Guidance

You are welcome to use and adapt OmicFlow for academic and research purposes. Please credit the original author and cite the GitHub repository.

Commercial use or major derivative projects should explicitly include attribution and comply with the MIT license terms.

My goal is to support the community, especially students and researchers who lack access to advanced data engineering resources — please share improvements openly and pay it forward.

---

## Quick Attribution Reminder

When using OmicFlow in publications, presentations, or derivative tools, please include:

> "Built using OmicFlow by Christopher Gaughan (2025). Available at: https://github.com/christophergaughan/OmicFlow"
